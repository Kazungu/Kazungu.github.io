This is a USSD/Shortcode service that enables SACCOs to register their members and collect savings via a mobile phone. Members can also access loans through our innovative platform.

We are changing the way SACCOs relate to their members and encouraging a culture of savings in Kenya by making it convenient.

Services

Member registration

Mobisave helps SACCOs register members on the web platform and also using the USSD Code *632#

Revenue collection

Members can make their contributions using mpesa and the transactions are viewed on the platform.

Analytics

Provides SACCOs with reports and analytics for informed decison making.
